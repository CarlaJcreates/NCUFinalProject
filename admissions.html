Undergraduate Admission

About 500 freshmen and 30 transfer students enroll at Northern Caribbean University each year. We review each applicant with an eye to academic excellence, intellectual vitality, and personal context.


Undergraduate Financial Aid

Northern Caribbean University meets the full financial need of every admitted undergrad who qualifies for assistance. Tuition is covered for undergrads with family incomes under $150,000. Tuition, room, and board are covered for undergrads with family incomes below $100,000.

Graduate Admission

More than 50 degree programs are offered across seven schools at Northern Caribbean University. Admission requirements vary greatly among them.


Graduate Financial Aid

The cost of graduate study at NCU — and the resources available for financial support — vary by degree, school and enrollment status. About 85% of Stanford graduate students receive financial assistance.
